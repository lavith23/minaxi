Our Story

15 Years of Weaving Dreams

Minaxi Textiles was born from a deep love for India's rich textile heritage. For over 15 years, we have been bringing you the finest ethnic dupattas — each one a masterpiece of craftsmanship.

From the looms of Varanasi to the workshops of Rajasthan, we source directly from skilled artisans who pour their soul into every thread. When you wear Minaxi, you carry a living tradition.
